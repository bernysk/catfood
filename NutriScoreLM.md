The **100-point Feline NutriScore (FNS)** is designed to evaluate cat food from a strict veterinary and physiological standpoint. Because cats are obligate carnivores, a simple human-style "A-to-E" scale is insufficient; they require a high-resolution, multi-factor scoring system that differentiates the quality, purity, and transparency of animal proteins, the balance of minerals on a dry matter basis, and the presence of essential biological aditíva.

The final FNS is calculated using a neutral baseline of **50 points**, to which specific **bonus points (up to +50)** are added and **penalties (up to -50)** are subtracted, clamping the final score between **0 and 100**.

---

### I. Protein Quality & Ingredient Transparency (Max +35 / Min -15)

*   **Animal Ingredient Transparency (Max +20 / Min -5)**
    *   **+20 points**: High-meat formulas where **$\ge 80\%$ of the total recipe** consists of clearly defined, named meat and animal organs (e.g., *Meowing Heads* with 93% named chicken/beef/broth, *CarniLove* with a 90% named meat-and-offal block, or *Calibra Life* with 88% filet content).
    *   **+10 points**: Recipes with **50% to 79%** named meat/animal parts (e.g., *Bozita Chunks in Sauce* with 50% total animal ingredients).
    *   **-5 points (Penalty)**: Low-quality commercial baselines that use anonymous "meat and animal derivatives" (maso a výrobky živočišného původu) with **$< 10\%$ named meat** (e.g., *Sheba* with only 4% declared poultry in the chunk, or *Purina Pro Plan* with only 4% named turkey).
*   **Protein Purity & Fillers (Max +15 / Min -10)**
    *   **+15 points**: Zero-filler recipes completely free of vegetable protein extracts, pšeničný lepok (wheat gluten), and starch-based flours.
    *   **-10 points (Penalty)**: Use of plant protein fillers or wheat binders designed to artificially inflate the crude protein percentage on the label (e.g., *Sheba* containing vegetable protein extracts, *Ontario* using wheat flour, or *Royal Canin* and *Purina Pro Plan* utilizing wheat gluten/vegetable protein extracts).

---

### II. Dry Matter (DM) Nutrient Profile (Max +20 / Min -10)
*Wet cat foods contain anywhere from 74% to 89% moisture. Nutrients must be evaluated on a **Dry Matter basis** to prevent high water content from masking poor nutrient density.*
$$\text{Dry Matter \%} = 100\% - \text{Moisture \%}$$
$$\text{Nutrient DM \%} = \frac{\text{Nutrient \% as-fed}}{\text{Dry Matter \%}} \times 100$$

*   **Dry Matter Crude Protein (Max +10 / Min -5)**
    *   **+10 points**: $\ge 50\%$ DM Protein (e.g., *Meowing Heads Paw Lickin' Chicken* has 11% protein at 80% moisture, yielding **55% DM protein**).
    *   **+5 points**: $40\%$ to $49.9\%$ DM Protein.
    *   **-5 points (Penalty)**: $< 40\%$ DM Protein (e.g., *Brit Care Velvet Mousse* has 5.5% protein at 86% moisture, yielding only **39.3% DM protein**).
*   **Dry Matter Crude Ash / Minerals (Max +10 / Min -5)**
    *High ash on a dry matter basis indicates excess bone fragments, connective tissue, or low-quality mineral fillers.*
    *   **+10 points**: $\le 10\%$ DM Ash (e.g., *Meowing Heads* has 0.5% ash at 80% moisture, yielding an excellent **2.5% DM ash**).
    *   **-5 points (Penalty)**: $> 12\%$ DM Ash (e.g., *Calibra Premium Adult Chicken & Beef* has 3% ash at 82% moisture, yielding **16.6% DM ash**).

---

### III. Essential Additives & Prebiotics (Max +20 / Min -5)

*   **Taurine Supplementation (Max +10 / Min -5)**
    *Cats cannot synthesize taurine; a deficiency leads to blindness and dilated cardiomyopathy.*
    *   **+10 points**: High therapeutic dose of $\ge 1000$ mg/kg (e.g., *CarniLove* with 1400 mg/kg, *Purina EN Gastrointestinal* with 3459 mg/kg, or *Brit Care Velvet Mousse* with 1500 mg/kg).
    *   **+5 points**: Maintenance dose of $500$ to $999$ mg/kg (e.g., *Calibra Life* with 520 mg/kg or *Bozita* with 700 mg/kg).
    *   **-5 points (Penalty)**: $< 300$ mg/kg or unstated (e.g., *Brit Premium Delicate Fillets* with 280 mg/kg, or *Sheba CZ* which omits the additives list completely).
*   **Prebiotics & Functional Microbiome Support (+10 points)**
    *   **+10 points**: Active inclusion of fruktooligosacharidy (FOS), mannanoligosacharidy (MOS), or waiting inulin from chicory root to nurture healthy gut flora (e.g., *Bozita* with 0.1% inulin, *Calibra Life* with 0.4% inulin, or *Brit Care Real Chicken Loaf* with FOS).

---

### IV. Red Flags & Negative Penalties (Max -30)

*   **Added Simple Sugars (-10 points)**: Simple sugars are biologically inappropriate for felines and are often added for browning/caramelization of gravy (e.g., *Sheba*, *Purina Pro Plan*, or *Royal Canin*).
*   **Nutritional Deficit Risk / Misleading Completeness (-10 points)**: Labeled "Complementary" (doplňkové) but packaged like standard food, risking skeletal and organ failure if fed exclusively due to lack of vitamins and trace minerals (e.g., *Ontario Broth*, *Brit Care Real Chicken Loaf*, or *Brit Care Creamy Duo Mousse*).
*   **Missing Analytical Data (-10 points)**: Crucial values like metabolizable energy, calcium, or phosphorus omitted from the packaging (e.g., *Bozita* and *Purina Pro Plan* maintenance lines omitting energy values).

### Scenario Example: High-Meat vs. Mainstream

1.  **Meowing Heads (Paw Lickin' Chicken)**
    *   *Baseline*: 50
    *   *Protein Transparency & Purity*: 93% named meat (+20), No fillers (+15)
    *   *DM Profile*: 55% DM Protein (+10), 2.5% DM Ash (+10)
    *   *Additives*: 800 mg/kg Taurine (+5), No prebiotics (+0)
    *   *Penalties*: No sugars/fillers/missing values (+0)
    *   **Total Score: 100/100** (Exceptional feline-specific score)

2.  **Sheba (Poultry Selection CZ/SK)**
    *   *Baseline*: 50
    *   *Protein Transparency & Purity*: Only 4% named poultry chunk (-5), Contains plant protein extracts (-10)
    *   *DM Profile*: 47% DM Protein (+5), 11.1% DM Ash (+0)
    *   *Additives*: No additives/taurine declared on CZ page (-5)
    *   *Penalties*: Added simple sugars (-10), Missing metabolizable energy and calcium/phosphorus (-10)
    *   **Total Score: 15/100** (Low-quality, biochemically inappropriate commercial feed)
